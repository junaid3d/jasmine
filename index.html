About Jasmine Learning

Learning that nurtures the whole child.

In today's world, children need more than worksheets, memorisation, and pressure-driven learning. They need spaces where they can ask questions, work with their hands, express themselves, and learn at a natural pace.

At Jasmine Learning, children learn through thoughtful questions, meaningful projects, hands-on discovery, and gentle guidance.

Our goal is simple: to make quality holistic and constructivist education accessible to more families at a reasonable price.

  • Think deeply
  • Build confidence
  • Develop kindness and resilience
  • Express ideas with courage
  • Learn in community

Learning Approach

Holistic constructivist learning.

Children do not simply receive information. They actively build understanding through experience, conversation, observation, creativity, and reflection.

The aim is not just to help children learn more. The aim is to help them become more thoughtful, confident, kind, and capable.

Our Philosophy

The sun is not only for a few.

At Jasmine Learning, we believe meaningful education should not belong only to the privileged. Knowledge, care, creativity, and emotional nourishment should be shared more generously.

A candle was never meant to be hidden. And the sun was never meant to belong to one person.

Jasmine Learning exists to create a warm, thoughtful, and accessible space where more children can grow in light.

For Parents

Is this right for your child?

Jasmine Learning is for families looking for calm, meaningful growth rather than pressure, rushing, or rote memorisation.

A good fit if you want your child to...

  • Become more confident
  • Ask better questions
  • Learn through projects instead of rote memorisation
  • Develop emotional resilience
  • Express ideas through art and discussion
  • Work kindly with other children
  • Build curiosity, independence, and self-awareness

What this is not

This is not a tuition centre. This is not a test-preparation programme.

This is a learning community designed to help children think, create, reflect, and grow in a calm, non-pressurised environment.
