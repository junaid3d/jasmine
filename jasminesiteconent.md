# Jasmine Learning

## A calm, curious learning studio for children

**Holistic. Constructivist. Community-based.**

At **Jasmine Learning**, children learn through thoughtful questions, meaningful projects, hands-on discovery, and gentle guidance.

We are a calm and organic learning space for children aged **3 to 12**, offering project-based programmes inspired by **Acton-style project journeys**, where learners explore big ideas through **Socratic discussions, creative expression, teamwork, and real-world challenges**.

Our goal is simple:

> To make quality holistic and constructivist education accessible to more families at a reasonable price.

---

## Learning that nurtures the whole child

In today’s world, children need more than worksheets, memorisation, and pressure-driven learning.

They need spaces where they can:

Think deeply.
Ask questions.
Build confidence.
Work with their hands.
Express themselves.
Develop kindness, resilience, and curiosity.
Learn at a natural pace.

**Jasmine Learning** is designed as a slow-paced, aesthetic, community-based studio where children feel safe, seen, and inspired.

---

## What makes Jasmine Learning different?

### Socratic Discussions

Children are encouraged to think, question, listen, and respond. Instead of being given all the answers, they learn how to explore ideas through guided discussion.

### Hands-on Projects

Every learning journey includes practical, creative, and meaningful projects that children can build, present, and reflect on.

### Project-Based Learning

Our programmes are built like learning adventures. Each project has a theme, a big question, challenges, missions, reflections, and a final expression of learning.

### A Calm Organic Environment

The studio setting is intentionally warm, calm, and aesthetic. We believe the environment plays an important role in how children feel and learn.

### Community-Based Learning

Children learn with others, not in isolation. They collaborate, share ideas, support one another, and become part of a kind learning community.

---

## Our age groups

### Early Learners

**Ages 3 to 7**

Gentle, playful, sensory-rich learning experiences for younger children. These programmes focus on curiosity, expression, confidence, storytelling, nature, movement, and early social development.

### Independent Learners

**Ages 8 to 12**

Project-based journeys for older children who are ready for deeper discussions, teamwork, problem-solving, research, creative projects, and meaningful reflection.

---

## Our learning approach

At Jasmine Learning, we follow a **holistic constructivist approach**.

This means children do not simply receive information. They actively build understanding through experience, conversation, observation, creativity, and reflection.

Our programmes may include:

* Socratic discussions
* Story-based learning
* Hands-on projects
* Art and creative expression
* Team missions
* Research-based challenges
* Reflection circles
* Exhibitions and presentations
* Real-world problem-solving
* Emotional and social development activities

The aim is not just to help children learn more.

The aim is to help them become more thoughtful, confident, kind, and capable.

# Our philosophy

## The sun is not only for a few

At Jasmine Learning, we believe meaningful education should not belong only to the privileged.

In today’s world, anything that nurtures the mind and heart, whether education, therapy, creativity, or emotional growth, is often made expensive and exclusive. But every child deserves to be seen, guided, and given the chance to grow.

We do not believe that some children deserve deeper learning simply because their families can afford it.

We believe knowledge, care, creativity, and emotional nourishment should be shared more generously.

A candle was never meant to be hidden.

And the sun was never meant to belong to one person.

**Jasmine Learning exists to create a warm, thoughtful, and accessible space where more children can grow in light.**
